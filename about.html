About

My name is Perry Mensah, and I’m a passionate visual storyteller studying Media & Communications at Texas Tech University. With a sharp eye for detail and a deep love for creative expression, I bring moments to life through film and photography.

Whether I’m capturing electrifying sports action, vibrant student events, or raw behind-the-scenes footage, I strive to craft stories that resonate. I believe every frame has power, and every project is an opportunity to connect people through emotion, rhythm, and vision.

From directing and shooting to editing and final delivery, I commit to bringing energy, creativity, and professionalism to every stage of the process.
